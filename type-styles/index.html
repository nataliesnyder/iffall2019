Demonstration of Type Stylings

Crimson Text

The typeface I am using is called Crimson Text
A sample size– what you are reading right now!– is 20px
The sample text I am using is:
“I was once told that there were only two men in my life: Jesus and Dracula. Shh! Now there's a third.”
Please note that this line is not of my own creation, but from a blog called Seeker of Truth, run by artist and writer Wendy Brydge. I encourage you to explore her world.
